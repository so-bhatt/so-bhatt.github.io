Soumyadeep Bhattacharjee is a High School student, who dreams of building a humanoid robot that will be smart enough to learn any repetitive tasks to support humans, while, the exclusive focus of the human race will be innovation and exploration within as well as beyond our universe. Soumyadeep is bestowed with an enormous passion for Mathematics and Computer Science. His present research interests lie in building intelligent Internet-Of-Things (IOT) systems for various real-world applications using innovative applications of Artificial Intelligence(AI), Machine Learning(ML), and Deep Learning(DL) Techniques.
